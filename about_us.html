About Us

Al Saqr National Library stands as the pinnacle of literary excellence in the UAE, centrally situated in Abu Dhabi and spanning over 3 branches throughout the region. Our shelves are consistently stocked with the freshest releases, aimed at fostering a love for literature among the younger generation. We firmly advocate for accessibility, particularly to students, as we believe it lays the groundwork for their future success and growth.
